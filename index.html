About Ellie May

Natural & Emotive
Wedding Photography

I am a Worcestershire-based wedding photographer dedicated to capturing the authentic, unscripted moments of your special day. My style blends documentary storytelling with artistic portraiture, ensuring your wedding gallery is a timeless reflection of your love.

From the nervous excitement of your morning preparations to the electric energy on the dance floor, I focus on the details that make your day unique. Whether you are hosting an intimate celebration in the Cotswolds or a grand affair across the UK, I am here to document your journey with care and creativity.
